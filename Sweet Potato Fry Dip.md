# Sweet Potato Fry Dip
This is halved for 2 person consumption, and it lasts for a good long while.

## Ingredients
* 1 1/4 cup mayonnaise
* 1 tbsp lime juice
* 1 tsp ground chipotle powder
* 1/2 tsp chili powder
* 1/4 tsp paprika
* 1/4 tsp cumin
* 1/4 tsp garlic powder
